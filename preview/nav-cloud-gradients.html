The “protruding” effect comes from the current recipe: a top white highlight + a bottom drop shadow, which together fake a raised dome. The alternatives drop or invert that:
G1 Flat & even – gentle top-to-bottom grey, no highlight. Calm and modern.
G2 Inset – inner top shadow makes the icon sit in a pressed well (iOS keycap feel).
G3 Top-lit halo – soft radial glow from the top, no hard edge. Premium, diffuse.
G4 Hairline ring – flat fill, shape read only from a crisp 1px inner border. Cleanest.
G5 Cool diagonal – soft diagonal with a faint cool tint, sits flush (no drop shadow).
