# Branche retag audit (s102, 2026-07-12)

The Branche filter overhaul (`docs/plans/BRANCHE_FILTER_OVERHAUL_PLAN.md` §4) re-tagged every
sector-tagged item under the new rule: **a tag means notably sector-specific (typically 1 to 4
sectors); applicable in 5+ sectors means untag (general, shows under every Branche).**
This report is the founder-review artifact: every changed item with old and new sectors plus a
one-line rationale, grouped by decision. Unchanged items are listed at the end for completeness.

Source register: 562 tagged items before the audit (393 words,
165 collocations, 4 texts).
