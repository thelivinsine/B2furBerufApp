Genauly

German for real life. Break through the B1 to B2 plateau.

Genauly is a German learning app for adults who already know the basics but feel stuck at the intermediate level, typically somewhere between B1 and B2. Instead of more grammar tables, it builds real confidence through the situations you actually face: a presentation at work, an appointment at the Behörde, a visit to the doctor, a job interview, or a difficult conversation with a colleague.

You practise branching dialogues, build vocabulary with spaced repetition, take leveled quizzes, and get AI feedback on your writing and pronunciation. The app also helps you prepare for the telc Deutsch B2 Beruf and Goethe-Zertifikat B2 exams. Signing in with Google is optional and is used only to create your account and save your learning progress across your devices. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.
